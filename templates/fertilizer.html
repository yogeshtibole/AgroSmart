📋 NPK Nutrient Guide
N
Nitrogen
Promotes leaf & stem growth. Gives dark green colour.
Sources: Urea (46%N), DAP (18%N), Ammonium Sulphate (21%N)
P
Phosphorus
Root development & flowering. Needed at sowing time.
Sources: DAP (46%P₂O₅), SSP (16%P), MOP
K
Potassium
Disease resistance, fruit quality, stress tolerance.
Sources: MOP (60%K₂O), SOP (50%K₂O), NPK complex
